Calculator Type Apple

This calculator is a copy of the calculator from the iPhone 13 mini version. It has some bugs, but it fulfills its function to the fullest. To change the degree of rounding, drag the slider to the desired side. Double-click on the output screen to delete the last value. The field on the left is for notes. The field on the right shows the history of the last calculations.

The bugs I found are:

  1. Infinite number of dots (but not in a row);
  2. Infinite zeros (e.g. writing the number 0000.01);
  3. Incorrect display of the calculation process during any operations, especially multiplication.

Despite these bugs, the calculation is correct.
